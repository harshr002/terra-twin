# TerraTwin – Problem Statement

## The Core Problem
Organizations that manage physical assets (construction sites, buildings,
infrastructure, land) operate with fragmented and delayed information.

Critical data is scattered:
- Spatial data in CAD/GIS tools
- Cost and material data in spreadsheets or ERPs
- Environmental data in static reports
- Visual site data stored manually

This leads to:
- Cost overruns
- Project delays
- Rework and waste
- Late discovery of structural or compliance risks

## Why Existing Solutions Fail
Current tools are:
- Narrow (only dashboards or only BIM)
- Expensive and license-locked
- Not predictive
- Not integrated into one live system

They answer:
> “What happened?”

They do NOT answer:
- What will happen?
- What should we do next?
- What is the optimal design or sequence?

## The Gap
There is no affordable, open, production-ready platform that:
- Creates a live digital twin of physical spaces
- Continuously updates with real-world signals
- Uses AI to predict risk, cost, and outcomes
- Works without vendor lock-in

## Why This Problem Is Urgent
- Infrastructure costs are rising
- Margins are shrinking
- Compliance pressure is increasing
- Decisions must be made faster than ever

Static reports are no longer enough.

## TerraTwin’s Role
TerraTwin exists to unify physical-world data into a single intelligent system
that enables prediction, simulation, and decision-making before mistakes happen.
